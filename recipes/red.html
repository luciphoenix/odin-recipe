RED RED

DESCRIPTION

Red Red is a hearty and flavorful dish made with black-eyed peas or cowpeas cooked in red palm oil and paired with fried ripe plantains. The dish gets its name from the red hue of the palm oil and the plantains. Itâ€™s vegetarian-friendly and packed with protein, making it both nutritious and delicious.

Ingredients

For the Beans Stew:

  • Black-eyed peas (cowpeas) - 2 cups (soaked overnight or pre-cooked)
  • Palm oil - 1/2 cup
  • Onions - 2 large (1 chopped, 1 blended)
  • Tomatoes - 4 medium-sized (chopped or blended)
  • Tomato paste - 2 tablespoons
  • Ginger - 1 thumb-sized piece (blended)
  • Garlic - 3 cloves (minced or blended)
  • Pepper (scotch bonnet or chili) - 1-2 (adjust to taste, blended)
  • Seasoning cube - 1
  • Salt - to taste
  • Smoked fish or shrimp (optional) - for added flavor

For the Fried Plantains:

  • Ripe plantains - 3 large
  • Vegetable oil - for frying
  • Salt - a pinch
